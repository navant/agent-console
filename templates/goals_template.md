> **How to use this template**
> Goals describe outcomes you want — not implementation steps. Keep them outcome-focused, measurable, and time-bound. Replace placeholders and remove guidance as you fill each section.

## Status & lifecycle

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `active` | Currently pursuing — eligible to run as a task |
| `paused` | Intentionally on hold |
| `achieved` | Outcome reached — keep for history |
| `abandoned` | No longer pursuing — note why in changelog |

Update the `status` field in frontmatter when the goal moves between stages.
